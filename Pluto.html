Pluto's Booty

This animatic was crafted in Procreate and composed in After Effects. It served as the final project for Word and Image 2, a studio offered at Washington University in St. Louis.

Character Line Up

For the prompt "Pluto Voyage," I created four characters for the space pirate adventure. Starky, the general and captain, leads the crew, accompanied by a robot, Wiffles, and a stowaway snake on the spaceship. The plot revolves around these space pirates discovering evil intentions within the loot they attained from planet Pluto, hidden in a treasured chest.

Character Drafts
