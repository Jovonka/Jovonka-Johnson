Post Apocalypse Girl

The Post Apocalypse Girl rig involved using Maya for modeling and rigging, and Substance Painter for texturing.

Body and Facial Rigging

In this project using Maya, I took on the challenge of rigging a post-apocalyptic girl, focusing on achieving realism and personality. The skinning process demanded meticulous attention to detail, ensuring each limb was accurately weighted for lifelike movement. Additionally, I delved into facial rigging basics, constantly refining expressions for a nuanced character. To elevate realism, I incorporated motion capture animation, seamlessly blending authentic movements with the character's style.
