Project 3: Remote

Created as a short animation film for a final project in Animated Worlds, a studio offered at Washington University in St. Louis. All aspects, excluding the Flour sack rig by Joe Daniels, were handled by me, including modeling, animating, texturing, lighting, rendering, and compositing.

Project 4: New York Buildings

Modeled based on my trip to New York City in Brooklyn, also exploring the use of Maya's toon shader. All aspects, including modeling, texturing, lighting, rendering, and compositing, were made by me.

Programs used: Maya, Substance Painter, Arnold.

Project 5: Rustic Reverie

Modeled with a stylized aesthetic inspired by Studio Ghibli movies, and stylized games. The project served as a testing ground for Maya's Python script to generate a wind effect animation. All aspects, including modeling, coding, texturing, lighting, rendering, and compositing, were handled by me.

Programs used: Maya, Substance Painter, Arnold.

Project 6: Star Quest

Figma App prototype created for a final project in Visual Principles for the Screen a studio at Washington University in St. Louis. Used Unreal Engine to create VFX materials for representing stars, and Maya for modeling and rigging the spaceman character. All aspects, including prototyping, modeling, texturing, lighting, VFX, and rendering, were completed by me.

Programs used: Figma, Maya, Unreal Engine 5, Arnold.
